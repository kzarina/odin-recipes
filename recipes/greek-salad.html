Greek Salad

Description

A very light salad made of fresh vegetables and cheese dressed with olive oil.

Ingredients:

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 large cucumber
  • salad leaves
  • 1/2 red onion
  • feta cheese
  • 4-5 olives
  • 1/2 lemon
  • salt
  • olive oil

Steps:

  1. Chop cucumber and salad leaves.
  2. Cut the tomatoes and feta cheese into cubes, olives into rings and lemon into small pieces.
  3. Slice the onion.
  4. Put everything into a bowl, add salt and olive oil and mix.
